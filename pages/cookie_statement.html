What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how the site is used.

How we use cookies

{{APP_NAME}} may use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies — required for core site functionality, such as security, navigation, and saving your display preferences (for example theme or layout settings stored locally in your browser).
  • Functional cookies — remember choices you make so the site works the way you expect.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve content and performance. Where we use third-party analytics, those providers may set their own cookies subject to their policies.

Managing cookies
