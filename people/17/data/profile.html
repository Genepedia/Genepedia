Nosekeni Nonqaphi Fanny Mandela

Nosekeni Nonqaphi Fanny Mandela Female Transkei, South Africa

Wife of Gadla Henry Mphakanyiswa Mandela

Mother of Nelson Mandela; Mabel Notancu Timakwe; and Constance Mbekeni Mandela

Nosekeni Nonqaphi Fanny Mandela was the mother of South African leader Nelson Mandela. Public biographies of Mandela often mention her as a steady influence during his childhood in the Transkei.
