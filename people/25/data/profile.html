Winnie Madikizela-Mandela

Ex-wife of Nelson Mandela (married 1958; divorced 1996)

Winnie Madikizela-Mandela was a South African activist and political figure. In Genepedia she is linked as the second wife of Nelson Mandela, with their marriage spanning most of his imprisonment years before their divorce.

Public life

Winnie Mandela played a prominent role in anti-apartheid politics and remained a nationally recognised figure both during and after Nelson Mandela's imprisonment. This profile currently focuses on documenting the marital link within the Mandela family pages.
