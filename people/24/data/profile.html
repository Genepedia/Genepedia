Evelyn Mase

Evelyn Ntoko Mase Female 1922

Ex-wife of Nelson Mandela (married 1944; divorced 1958)

Evelyn Mase was the first wife of Nelson Mandela. They married in 1944 and later divorced as Mandela's political work and periods of arrest increasingly shaped family life.
