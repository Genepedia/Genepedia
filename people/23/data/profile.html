Graça Machel Female October 17, 1945
Mozambique

Wife of Nelson Mandela

Graça Machel is a Mozambican humanitarian and public figure. In Genipedia she is linked as the third wife of Nelson Mandela, whom she married in 1998.

Public life

Machel is widely known for work connected to children's rights, education, and development initiatives in southern Africa. This profile currently focuses on documenting her connection within the Mandela family pages.
