Gadla Henry Mphakanyiswa Mandela

Gadla Henry Mphakanyiswa Mandela was a Thembu leader and community councillor in the Transkei. He is best known as the father of Nelson Mandela.

Role in the Thembu community

Biographical accounts of Nelson Mandela describe Gadla as a local chief (or headman) in Mvezo and a counsellor within the Thembu royal sphere. He died when Nelson was still a child, after which extended family within the Thembu household helped shape Nelson's upbringing.
